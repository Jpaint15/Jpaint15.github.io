The CRAP principles are the principles of design using html code. The 4 principles are contrast,
alignment, repetition, and proximity. Contrast is the concept that different colors, shapes, and
themes to draw the reader's attention. Repetition is like consistency and readers tend to be more
invested i what their reading if they see continued patterns. Alignment control's the reader's eyes
and what their eyes go to and influences their opinion if things are clean or not. Proximity help's
organize their ewbsite by keeping similar elements near one another creating a nice flow for the website
