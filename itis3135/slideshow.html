Top 6 NBA Players For the 2022 Season



Giannis Antetoutmpo

Giannis is coming off his first championship run where he dominated on both end of the floor
during the 2021 playoffs. He looks to repeat with the Milwuakke Bucks and prove why he thinks he
deserves to be known as the #1 player in the NBA.

Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant is coming off a comeback year for the ages where he was amazing in the 2021 NBA
playoffs. KD and the Nets came up just short as injuries caught up to them and KD looks for his
3rd NBA championship and 1st with the Brooklyn Nets.

Lebron James

Lebron James is going into his 19th year of his illustrias career. Lebron will be going for
his 5th NBA championship on his quest for NBA dominance.

Steph Curry

Steph Curry is coming off a bounce back year in 2021 where he was ofrced to carry a Golden State Team
that was expecting Klay to come back but then tore his ACL in the offeseason. Curry looks for a
repeat of what happened last year but with more wins as the Warriors expect to be much more healhy.

Kawhi Leonard

Kawhi Leonard tore his ACL in the Semifinals of last year's playoffs and will be out for much
of the year as he looks to recover. Kawhi was phenomenol before he got injured in the playoffs
Kawhi is known as a top 5 player around the league and one of the best two-ways in the league.

Luka Doncic

Luka is coming off a great playoffs last year trying to single-handedly carry his team past the
better L.A. Clippers. The Mavericks had new HC Jason Kidd look to have a much more improved year this year which could lead the way for Luka to win his first MVP.
